PlatOps Audit Tool

Mission Statement

This audit framework will allow Dstillery to turn platform knowledge into business performance. By maintaining consistent oversight across DSPs and SSPs, we identify risks early, surface competitive insights, and ensure our products deliver value at every point of activation. Through a structured approach to the buyer journey, audit findings help us close gaps and continuously strengthen how our solutions are activated and delivered.

Strategic pillars

Platform Fluency
Deepen expertise across every DSP and SSP. The goal is a team equipped to troubleshoot issues, identify opportunities, and deliver confident guidance on Dstillery solutions across all platforms.
Test-And-Learn
Systematically evaluate Dstillery solutions to validate consistency, identify platform-specific advantages and limitations, and surface actionable insights that inform where and how we activate.
Audience Quality
Maintain audience integrity across platforms through consistent taxonomy, validated ingestion, and structured audits that surface and resolve data quality issues before they impact delivery.

Audit Process

1
Monitor Stay ahead of platform changes
Track platform changes, UI updates, and workflow shifts across every DSP and SSP we operate on.
2
Audit Protect product integrity
Run structured checks on audience integrity, deal discoverability, and segment performance across all platforms.
3
Report Turn findings into decisions
Log findings to the Audit Log and surface insights that inform smarter activation decisions across DSPs and SSPs.
